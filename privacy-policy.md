# Privacy Policy for GreenBus

**Last Updated: February 12, 2026**

## Introduction

GreenBus ("we," "our," or "us") operates a mobile application for bus ticket booking services in Cameroon. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information You Provide

When you use GreenBus, we collect the following personal information:

- **Account Information**: Name, email address, phone number
- **Booking Information**: Passenger names, contact details, travel dates, routes, seat preferences
- **Payment Information**: Payment method details (processed securely through third-party payment processors)
- **Profile Information**: Saved favorite routes, travel preferences, loyalty points

### 2. Information Collected Automatically

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: App interactions, feature usage, booking history
- **Location Data**: Departure and destination cities selected for bookings (we do not track real-time GPS location)

### 3. Information from Third Parties

- **Payment Processors**: Transaction confirmation and payment status
- **Authentication Providers**: If you sign in using Google, we receive basic profile information (name, email)

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

### Service Delivery
- Process and manage your bus ticket bookings
- Generate digital tickets with QR codes
- Send booking confirmations and trip updates
- Manage seat assignments and availability

### Customer Support
- Respond to your inquiries and support requests
- Process refund requests
- Resolve booking issues

### Service Improvement
- Analyze usage patterns to improve app functionality
- Develop new features and services
- Optimize route offerings based on demand

### Communications
- Send transactional notifications (booking confirmations, trip reminders)
- Provide customer support updates
- Send promotional offers for flash deals (with your consent)

### Security and Fraud Prevention
- Verify user identity during check-in
- Prevent fraudulent bookings and duplicate tickets
- Ensure secure payment processing

## Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Compliance)

We process your personal data based on:

- **Contract Performance**: Processing necessary to fulfill our booking services
- **Legitimate Interests**: Improving services, preventing fraud, customer support
- **Consent**: Marketing communications (you can opt out anytime)
- **Legal Obligation**: Compliance with applicable laws and regulations

## How We Share Your Information

### Service Providers
We share data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us:

- **OnSpace Cloud**: Backend infrastructure and database hosting
- **Payment Processors**: Secure payment processing (Mobile Money, card payments)
- **Email Service Providers**: Transactional email delivery

### Business Partners
- **Bus Operators**: Share passenger information necessary for trip execution and check-in

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government regulation.

### Business Transfers
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

## We Do NOT Sell Your Data
GreenBus does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption**: Data transmitted using TLS/SSL encryption
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access restrictions for staff (Super Admin, Manager, Driver)
- **Secure Storage**: Database hosted on secure cloud infrastructure with encryption at rest
- **QR Code Security**: Unique tokens prevent ticket fraud and duplication
- **Regular Audits**: Security assessments and vulnerability testing

## Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

- Fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy
- Comply with legal obligations (e.g., tax records, refund processing)
- Resolve disputes and enforce our terms

**Retention Periods:**
- **Active Accounts**: Data retained while your account is active
- **Booking Records**: Retained for 3 years after trip completion
- **Payment Records**: Retained for 7 years (tax compliance)
- **Deleted Accounts**: Personal data deleted within 30 days (excluding legal requirements)

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

### Access and Portability
- Request a copy of your personal data
- Export your booking history

### Correction
- Update or correct inaccurate information via your profile settings

### Deletion
- Request deletion of your account and personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)

### Objection
- Opt out of marketing communications
- Object to processing based on legitimate interests

### Restriction
- Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances

### Withdrawal of Consent
- Withdraw consent for marketing communications anytime

**To exercise your rights, contact us at:** privacy@greenbus.cm

## Children's Privacy

GreenBus is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we have inadvertently collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

## International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside Cameroon where our service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

## Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The GreenBus mobile app does not use cookies. We may use local storage and analytics tools to:

- Remember your preferences
- Analyze app usage patterns
- Improve performance

You can control analytics tracking in your device settings.

## Third-Party Links

Our app may contain links to third-party services (e.g., payment processors, app stores). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. Please review their privacy policies before providing any information.

## Push Notifications

With your permission, we send push notifications for:

- Booking confirmations
- Trip reminders and updates
- Flash deal alerts

You can disable push notifications in your device settings or app preferences.
